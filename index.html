Welcome to Xun Dao

Welcome to Xun Dao, a Global Game Jam 2022 creation with the theme of "Duality". 🌟 Experience the power of Yin and Yang as you battle unique monsters. 🐉

Maintain balance between Yin and Yang while attacking enemies with three types of attacks:

  • Normal Attack - Basic attack with neutral effect ⚔️
  • Yin Attack - Increases Yin power 🌙
  • Yang Attack - Increases Yang power ☀️

Your goal: Defeat all monsters while managing your Yin and Yang levels to maintain balance. 🏆

Controls:

  • WASD - Move 🕹️
  • Q/E - Adjust difficulty at bonfire 🔥
  • Q/E - Use Yin/Yang skills 🌙☀️
  • Space - Dash 💨

Are you ready to embrace the challenge and harness the power of Yin and Yang to emerge victorious? ⚔️🌟
